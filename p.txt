CURRENT BUG
In Listening mode:
1. The quiz starts correctly with answer options disabled.
2. I press Play and the browser voice plays.
3. The options may look enabled or may remain visually disabled, but I cannot select/click any answer.
4. Therefore the Listening quiz cannot progress.

The expected behavior is:
- Before the first successful audio play: answer options must be disabled.
- After the first successful audio play: answer options must become enabled and clickable immediately.
- One click must register exactly one answer, show feedback, stop active speech, and auto-advance after about 800ms.
- Questions 2 and 3 under the same clip must remain answerable without requiring another play.
- A new clip must again require at least one successful Play before its first answer can be selected.
